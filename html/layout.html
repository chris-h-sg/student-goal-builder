How to Use

Welcome to the Report Writer!

I developed this tool to make report writing easier and less time-consuming, while still flexible enough to create personal, individualized comments. The comments are closely tied to standards and expectations for children in grades K-2. They are organized by subject.

The inspiration for these comments comes from the Common Core, AERO Standards, the IB Primary Years Program, 6+1 Traits, the work of Fountas and Pinnell, as well as reports Iâ€™ve written in the past.

How to use:

  • On the right side, enter the student's name and gender.
    This name will always appear in the first sentence. Thereafter, if possible the correct gender will be automatically applied.
  • Select comments that apply for each subject area. Open subject areas by clicking on them.
    General statements
    • It has been a real pleasure working with [Name] this .
  • A paragraph will be generated in the box on the right.
    53Characters
    10Words
    If you decide not to use a comment you already selected, click on the comment again and it will be removed.
  • When you're ready, copy and paste the paragraph into your report document. Adjust comments as needed so that the writing is fluid and paints a picture of a specific child.
